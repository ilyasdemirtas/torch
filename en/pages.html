OUR STORY

The story of Torch Brewery is a tale of passion, belief, and a bold vision…

In 2014, when we embarked on our journey at the historic Bomonti Brewery in the heart of Istanbul, we dedicated ourselves to creating Turkey's finest and most meticulous beers

We view brewing as an art form. That's why we always prepare all our beers with the finest ingredients and our unique yeasts.

This creative process, which we advance with great enthusiasm and joy at every stage, has led us to embrace the ’small batch’ production philosophy. Thanks to this approach, we can offer nearly 30 different styles of beer each year and constantly pursue innovations and improvements in our recipes.

Thanks to this approach, we've already begun to hang the first awards from international beer competitions on our walls in the brewery…

In 2023, with the new production facility we established in Izmir by reviving the Historic Bomonti-Nectar Brewery, we continue to rejuvenate the brewing heritage of this land…

Our priority is always to touch the hearts of beer lovers by creating delicious and flavorful beers, and to be a pioneer and a friend of Turkey's burgeoning craft beer industry.

OUR MISSION
  • Producing the finest beers in the region at our breweries in Istanbul and Izmir's Historic Bomonti Breweries, leading the way in Turkey's award-winning beer culture.
  • Offering nearly 30 different beer styles with our 'small batch' production approach, advancing Turkey's beer culture with rich and gourmet experiences.
  • Embracing a production process that is environmentally friendly and sustainable, following principles of transparency and accountability.
  • Acting with a sense of social responsibility and awareness, emphasizing values of equality and diversity.
  • Implementing a zero-waste policy and demonstrating an environmentally conscious approach throughout the production process.
  • Continuing to add value to the craft beer industry in Turkey by taking on a leading and pioneering role.
OUR PHILOSOPHY

Torch Brewery continues to exist to enrich the craft beer culture in Turkey.

Aware of the fact that beer enthusiasts in Turkey are an enlightened and conscious community, we follow a perfectionist production process.

The heritage of the historic breweries where we produce is a constant source of pride and inspiration for us.

By blending ancient beer-making knowledge with modern craft beer artisanship, we work diligently and enthusiastically to create a brand-new story in every kettle we boil.
